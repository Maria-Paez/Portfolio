About us

Who are we?

The South Florida Immigrant Protection (SFIP) is a non-profit organization committed to upholding fairness and justice for immigrants in the United States, with a particular focus on the South Florida region. Our mission is to provide comprehensive support and legal assistance to immigrants facing challenging circumstances. Through our team of experienced attorneys and dedicated volunteers, we offer pro-bono legal representation to individuals in need. We understand the difficulties and complexities immigrants often encounter, and we strive to ensure they have access to quality legal services regardless of their financial situation.

In addition to our direct legal services, SFIP is actively involved in legal training programs for both lawyers and community members. We believe that empowering individuals with legal knowledge and resources enhances their ability to navigate the complex immigration system effectively. SFIP is also committed to advocating for immigrant rights at the federal level. We work tirelessly to promote fair immigration policies, fight against discrimination, and push for comprehensive immigration reform. By engaging in policy advocacy, public education, and collaboration with other organizations, we aim to create a more inclusive and just society for immigrants.

At SFIP, we firmly believe in the importance of creating a supportive and welcoming environment for immigrants. We strive to build strong partnerships with local communities, organizations, and stakeholders to foster a sense of belonging and provide a network of support. Through our collective efforts, we seek to make a meaningful and lasting impact on the lives of immigrants, ensuring they are treated with dignity, respect, and equality under the law.
